# Student Business Project — ARVE Air (IoT for Luxury Hospitality)

## Context
As part of my studies at **EHL (École hôtelière de Lausanne)**, I worked on an 8-week **Student Business Project (SBP)** in collaboration with **ARVE Air**, a digital services brand of the Swiss startup **Dragonback Innovation AG**, with operations in Switzerland and Hong Kong.

The project focused on applying business and market analysis to a real B2B technology solution in the hospitality industry and received the **highest possible grade (6/6)**.

---

## Project Objective
Design a **marketing and business development strategy** for **ARVE Air’s IoT-based digital services**, targeting **upper-upscale and luxury hotels** in **Tier-1 cities across Europe and the Americas**.

ARVE Air’s solution is based on:
- In-room **IoT sensor units**
- Real-time **air quality data collection**
- **Data analytics and machine learning** to enable digital services such as:
    - Smoker detection
    - Housekeeping optimization
    - Indoor air quality monitoring
    - Operational efficiency insights for hotel management

---

## My Role
**Business & Market Analyst / Strategy Consultant (Student Project)**

I contributed across the full project lifecycle, including:
- Market and trend research
- Competitive and systems landscape analysis
- Market segmentation and profiling
- Business development and go-to-market strategy design
- Partner and channel strategy definition

---

## Deliverables & Work Performed

### 1. Market & Trend Analysis
Conducted an extensive **secondary research study** focused on:
- Guest expectations in luxury hospitality  
  (personalization, digital convenience, hygiene, sustainability)
- In-room technology and IoT adoption
- SPA, wellness, and health trends
- Impact of COVID-19 on hotel operations and guest behavior

The analysis was performed **city-by-city** across Europe and the Americas, covering:
- Occupancy rates and room supply
- Presence of SPA, restaurants, and meeting spaces
- Air quality indicators and 5G connectivity readiness
- Dominant hotel brands and “trophy hotels”

---

### 2. Hotel Systems Landscape Analysis
Mapped and analyzed the **core software ecosystem** used in hotel operations, including:
- Property Management Systems (PMS)
- Housekeeping systems
- SPA & wellness systems
- Preventive maintenance systems (CMMS)
- Central Reservation Systems (CRS)

**Key insight:**  
Hotels using **open-API architectures** are significantly better positioned to integrate IoT services and unlock operational and data-driven value.

---

### 3. Market Segmentation & Targeting
Identified and profiled high-potential customer segments, including:
- Global luxury and upper-upscale hotel chains
- Independent luxury hotels and resorts
- Regional and international hotel groups
- Properties with a strong focus on wellness, hygiene, and operational efficiency

---

### 4. Business Development Strategy
Designed a **phased go-to-market strategy**:

#### Short-term
- Partnerships with consulting firms, housekeeping associations, and hospitality tech associations
- Positioning around hygiene, safety, and COVID-driven operational needs

#### Medium-term
- Collaboration with hotelier associations and regional brand offices
- Expansion through trusted industry intermediaries

#### Long-term
- Strategic partnerships with global hotel chains and architects
- Early integration of IoT solutions at the hotel design and renovation stage
